Welcome to Vanguard Network, the ultimate hub for gamers of all levels and backgrounds! Whether you're a casual player looking for a laid-back gaming session or a competitive Esports enthusiast aiming for the top, you've found the right place. Who We Are We are a passionate community of gamers who believe in the power of video games to connect people from around the world. Our goal is to create an inclusive and engaging space where players can team up, compete, and share their gaming experiences.

What We Offer:

Active Community – Join discussions, make friends, and find teammates for your favourite games.

Events – Participate in regular game nights, and special events.

Support & Collaboration – Whether you're a streamer, content creator, or just a passionate gamer, we provide support to help you grow.

Our Store – Check out our store for products within FiveM with expansion for other games coming soon!

Dedicated Game Servers – Check out our dedicated server for ArmA 3, with more games we plan to host coming soon!

Partner With Us - Content creator or developer? Partner with us and help us continue to grow Vanguard!

Join Us Today! Be a part of a thriving gaming family! Whether you're here to play, compete, or just hang out, we welcome you with open arms. Connect with us on our forums, Discord, and social media to stay updated and get involved.

Let’s game together and build an epic community!
